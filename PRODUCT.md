# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Solo lifters who train with structured routines. They are on the gym floor, often one-handed, between sets, with limited attention. They need to log reps, weight, and completion quickly without breaking flow, while still getting enough structure (rest timers, exercise order, cues) to stay on track.

## Product Purpose

Gymbo is an AI-assisted workout companion that pairs a first-class routine logger with an equal-weight coaching chat. Success is a completed session where every set was logged with almost no friction, rest and structure kept the lifter on rhythm, and the app surfaced meaningful moments (PRs, trends, coach-like feedback) without turning the workout into a dashboard or spreadsheet.

## Brand Personality

Focused, calm, precise. The interface should feel like a fast tool that disappears into the workout: confident, minimal, expert. Motivation comes from clarity and well-timed insight, not hype or bro-gym energy.

## Anti-references

- Generic SaaS dashboards: hero metrics, identical card grids, gradient accents, decorative stats that restate the obvious.
- Spreadsheet and admin UIs: dense tables, grid-first layouts, and chrome that treats logging like data entry instead of a physical workout rhythm.
- Bro-gym aesthetics: neon, aggressive type, stock-photo motivation, loud visual noise on the floor.
- Default AI chatbot chrome: purple gradients, glassmorphism, generic "What can I help with?" emptiness that competes with the workout.

## Design Principles

1. **Log at the speed of a set.** The set row is the atomic unit; inputs, completion, and navigation must work one-handed without modal detours.
2. **Structure is silent until needed.** Timers, exercise order, and cues guide flow without nagging or blocking the next rep.
3. **Insight arrives in context.** PRs, trends, and coach feedback show up when they change behavior or celebrate a moment, not as always-on dashboard filler.
4. **Earned familiarity over novelty.** Interaction patterns should feel as intentional as Linear or Raycast: dense, fast, predictable, no invented affordances for standard tasks.
5. **Chat and routine are peers.** Neither surface is a sidebar afterthought; layout and hierarchy treat coaching dialogue and live logging as equal partners in a session.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

No formal compliance mandate yet. Default to sensible product accessibility: WCAG 2.1 AA contrast where practical, visible focus states, keyboard paths for chat and navigation, and `prefers-reduced-motion` respect. Favor large touch targets and readable type for gym-floor glare and one-handed use as the product hardens.
